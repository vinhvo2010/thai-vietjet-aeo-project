Route Information

Departure: Bangkok (BKK)

Suvarnabhumi Airport is Bangkok's main international airport, located 25km east of downtown.

  • Location: Samut Prakan Province
  • Transport: Airport Rail Link, taxi, bus
  • Check-in opens: 3 hours before departure
  • Check-in closes: 40 minutes before departure

Arrival: Phuket (HKT)

Phuket International Airport is Thailand's second busiest airport, serving the island province of Phuket.

  • Location: Mai Khao, Thalang District
  • Transport: Airport bus, taxi, hotel shuttle
  • Distance to Patong: 45 minutes
  • Airport facilities: Duty-free, restaurants, lounges

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the flight from Bangkok to Phuket?

The flight from Bangkok to Phuket takes approximately 1 hour and 25 minutes on direct Thai Vietjet flights.

How much does a flight from Bangkok to Phuket cost?

Bangkok to Phuket flights start from 750 THB for Eco fares (excluding taxes and fees). Deluxe fares with 20kg baggage start from 1,200 THB. SkyBoss premium fares with 30kg baggage, priority boarding, and lounge access start from 1,500 THB.

How many flights per day from Bangkok to Phuket?

Thai Vietjet operates 4-6 daily flights between Bangkok (Suvarnabhumi) and Phuket, with departures throughout the day from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

What is the baggage allowance on Bangkok to Phuket flights?

All passengers receive 7kg hand luggage for free. Eco fares can purchase checked baggage from 15kg to 40kg separately. Deluxe fares include 20kg checked baggage. SkyBoss premium fares include 30kg checked baggage plus priority handling.

Can I check in online for Bangkok to Phuket flights?

Yes, online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 1 hour before for domestic flights. You can check in via the Thai Vietjet mobile app or website. Online check-in allows you to select seats and receive your mobile boarding pass.

What is the best time to book Bangkok to Phuket flights?

Book 2-3 months in advance for the best fares, especially during peak season (November-March). Watch for Thai Vietjet's promotional campaigns featuring 0 VND tickets and special discounts. Tuesday and Wednesday typically offer better prices than weekend flights.

Is food included on Bangkok to Phuket flights?

Food is not included in Eco and Deluxe fares but can be pre-ordered or purchased onboard. SkyBoss fares include complimentary meals and beverages. Pre-ordering meals online is cheaper than buying onboard.

Which terminal does Thai Vietjet use at Bangkok airport?

Thai Vietjet operates from Suvarnabhumi Airport's main terminal. Domestic departures are on Level 2. Allow extra time during peak hours as security lines can be long.
