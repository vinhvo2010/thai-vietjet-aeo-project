Project Overview

This project aims to transform Thai Vietjet's digital presence through AI-First Marketing and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), positioning the airline for success in an AI-driven travel discovery landscape where ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity are becoming primary research tools for 50%+ of travelers.

Key Objectives: Increase AI visibility from <10% to 50-70%, capture more direct bookings, reduce dependence on OTAs, and establish Thai Vietjet as the authoritative source for Thailand-Vietnam-Australia routes in AI-powered search.

Key Research Findings

📊

AI Search is Transforming Travel Planning

50%+ of travelers now use AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) for trip planning. 13% of all searches now show AI-generated answers. By 2027, AI-driven traffic is expected to overtake traditional search.

🎯

91% of Travelers Have No Preferred Airline

When travelers start researching flights, 91% don't have a specific airline in mind. This creates a massive opportunity for airlines that appear prominently in AI-generated travel recommendations.

🔧

Schema Markup Drives Visibility

Airlines implementing Flight schema, FAQPage schema, and HowTo schema see up to 75% SEO visibility in their markets (e.g., Avianca in Latin America). Virgin Australia achieved an average rank of 1.3 for non-branded queries through route-page optimization.

⚡

Content Quality Matters for AI

AI systems prioritize structured, answerable content with clear E-E-A-T signals (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness). Airlines publishing destination guides, FAQs, and route-specific content capture traffic long before travelers are ready to book.

📈

Early Adopters Win

Regional airlines implementing AEO strategies see 60-70% increase in organic traffic within 6 months. Fly ALS (Kenya) increased traffic from 1,200 to 2,050 visitors/month (+70%) and direct bookings by 28% through comprehensive SEO + AEO implementation.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)

Technical Setup & Quick Wins

  • Implement Organization schema for Thai Vietjet
  • Add Flight schema to existing route pages
  • Create and implement llms.txt file
  • Setup GA4 tracking for AI referrals (filter for "chat" source)
  • Optimize 5 key pages with AEO format (TL;DR, Q&A headings)
  • Create FAQPage schema for top 10 FAQs
Deliverables: Organization schema, Flight schema on 5 routes, llms.txt, GA4 setup, 10 FAQs with schema
Phase 2: Content Creation (Months 2-3)

Route Pages & Destination Guides

  • Create 25 route-specific landing pages (all Thai Vietjet routes)
  • Publish 10 destination guides (Phuket, Chiang Mai, Da Nang, Hanoi, etc.)
  • Create 20 FAQ pages (baggage, check-in, booking, refunds, etc.)
  • Implement Flight + Offer schema on all route pages
  • Add TouristAttraction + TouristDestination schema to guides
  • Create HowTo schema for check-in and booking processes
Deliverables: 25 route pages, 10 destination guides, 20 FAQ pages, all with proper schema markup
Phase 3: AI Optimization (Months 4-5)

GEO & Off-Site Presence

  • Optimize content for conversational queries (long-tail, 6+ words)
  • Build presence on Reddit (r/travel, r/Thailand, r/Vietnam)
  • Answer questions on Quora about Thailand-Vietnam flights
  • Guest posts on travel blogs (5-10 articles)
  • Digital PR campaign for brand mentions
  • Monthly AI brand tests (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI)
Deliverables: 50+ Reddit/Quora answers, 5-10 guest posts, monthly AI visibility reports
Phase 4: Monitoring & Scaling (Month 6+)

Continuous Optimization

  • Weekly AI brand tests across all major platforms
  • Monthly content updates (refresh FAQs, add new routes)
  • A/B testing of content formats and structures
  • Expand to emerging AI platforms (Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  • Scale content library (100+ pages total)
  • Build backlink portfolio (target: 100+ quality links)
Deliverables: Weekly reports, monthly optimization updates, quarterly strategy reviews
