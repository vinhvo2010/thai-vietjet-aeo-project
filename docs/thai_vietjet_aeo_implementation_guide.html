AEO Strategy for Airlines: Industry Best Practices

Why AEO Matters for Airlines

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in blue links. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) focuses on being cited in AI-generated answers. The difference is critical:

  • 91% of travelers don't have a preferred airline when they start searching
  • AI search is replacing traditional search – Google AI Overviews appear in 13% of searches
  • ChatGPT has 400M weekly users asking travel questions
  • By 2028, 75% of searches will be AI-powered
  • If you're not in the AI answer, you're invisible

Core AEO Principles for Airlines

1. Query-Focused Content

Create content that directly answers traveler questions:

  • ✓ "What is the cheapest airline from Bangkok to Phuket?"
  • ✓ "How much baggage can I bring on Thai Vietjet?"
  • ✓ "Does Thai Vietjet fly to Australia?"
  • ✗ Generic "About Us" or "Services" pages

2. Structured Data

Implement schema markup so AI can understand your content:

  • Flight schema: flight numbers, times, prices
  • FlightReservation schema: booking confirmations
  • FAQPage schema: common questions
  • HowTo schema: check-in, baggage procedures

3. Route-Specific Pages

Every route should have a crawlable landing page:

  • Example: "Bangkok to Phuket Flights"
  • Include: frequency, duration, price range, aircraft type
  • Add: destination guide, booking CTA, FAQ
  • Virgin Australia did this → 27% share of voice

4. E-E-A-T Signals

Build Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust:

  • Author bylines (e.g., "Written by Captain John Smith")
  • Customer reviews with Review schema
  • Awards & certifications
  • Active social media presence

Template 3: FAQ Page

Thai Vietjet Baggage Policy - FAQs

How much baggage can I bring on Thai Vietjet?

All passengers can bring 7kg of cabin baggage (dimensions: 56cm x 36cm x 23cm). Checked baggage depends on fare type: Eco (no checked baggage), Deluxe (20kg), SkyBoss (30kg).

Can I purchase additional baggage?

Yes, you can add checked baggage when booking or later via "Manage Booking". Prices start at 200 THB for 15kg on domestic flights, 400 THB for 15kg on international flights.

What items are prohibited in cabin baggage?

Liquids over 100ml, sharp objects, flammable items, and weapons are prohibited. Laptops and power banks must be in cabin baggage, not checked baggage.

What happens if my baggage exceeds the limit?

Excess baggage fees apply: 500 THB per kg on domestic flights, 1,000 THB per kg on international flights. Pay at the airport check-in counter.

Can I bring sports equipment or musical instruments?

Yes, but they count toward your baggage allowance. Oversized items (surfboards, golf clubs) may require special handling fees. Contact customer service in advance.
