TRIỂN KHAI KỸ THUẬT - SCHEMA MARKUP

Schema Types cần triển khai

1. Flight Schema

Ưu tiên: CRITICAL

Pages: Route pages (Bangkok-Phuket, Bangkok-Hanoi, etc.)

Properties: flightNumber, departureAirport, arrivalAirport, departureTime, price, offers

2. FlightReservation Schema

Ưu tiên: HIGH

Pages: Booking confirmation, email

Properties: reservationId, passengerName, boardingGroup, totalPrice

3. FAQPage Schema

Ưu tiên: CRITICAL

Pages: FAQ pages, support pages

Impact: Direct answers in AI responses

4. Organization Schema

Ưu tiên: HIGH

Pages: Homepage, about page

Properties: logo, contactPoint, sameAs (social media)

5. HowTo Schema

Ưu tiên: MEDIUM

Pages: Check-in guide, baggage guide, travel documents

Impact: Step-by-step AI guidance

6. Offer Schema

Ưu tiên: MEDIUM

Pages: Promotions, special deals

Properties: price, validFrom, validThrough, availability

Deliverable: Đã chuẩn bị đầy đủ JSON-LD templates cho tất cả schema types → Ready for implementation trong Phase 1

Content Principles

  • Conversational: Write for AI questions

  • Comprehensive: Answer all related questions
  • Accurate: Up-to-date information, prices

AI-Optimization Tips

  • Use question-based headings (H2, H3)
  • Include TL;DR summaries at top
  • Add comparison tables
  • Embed schema in every page

ĐÁNH GIÁ RỦI RO

Rủi ro & Phương án giảm thiểu

Timeline Delays

Risk: Content creation hoặc technical implementation chậm trễ

Mitigation: Phased approach với clear milestones; prioritize critical items (Flight schema, top route pages); có backup resources nếu cần

AI Algorithm Changes

Risk: ChatGPT, Perplexity thay đổi cách hiển thị citations

Mitigation: Focus on fundamentals (quality content, structured data, E-E-A-T); diversify across multiple AI platforms; monitor changes monthly

Resource Constraints

Risk: Thiếu dev resources hoặc content writers

Mitigation: Templates đã sẵn sàng để developers implement nhanh; consider external content agency nếu cần; train internal team trên AEO best practices

ROI Timeline

Risk: Results có thể mất 6-9 tháng để thấy rõ

Mitigation: Set realistic expectations; track leading indicators (schema coverage, content published, AI citations); celebrate early wins (first AI citations, traffic improvements)

Competitor Response

Risk: AirAsia, Nok Air bắt đầu AEO optimization

Mitigation: First-mover advantage rất quan trọng; continuous improvement để maintain lead; focus on unique value props (SkyBoss, Boeing fleet, PowerPass)

Overall Risk Level: LOW-MEDIUM. Tất cả rủi ro đều manageable với proper planning và execution. Benefits vượt xa risks.
